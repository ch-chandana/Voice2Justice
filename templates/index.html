For Citizens

Speak freely. Voice2Justice does the legal translation for you.

  • Submit complaints in plain language — no legal knowledge required
  • Accessible to rural, elderly, and first-time complainants
  • Instant complaint ID & real-time status tracking
  • Auto-routed to the exact authority — zero guesswork

For Authorities

Receive structured, actionable intelligence — not raw noise.

  • Pre-structured FIR drafts with BNS legal codes mapped
  • Complaints auto-classified as Criminal or Civic before arrival
  • Automated priority triage — High / Medium severity labels
  • Email alerts dispatched instantly to the right department

Governance Impact

Smarter data. Faster justice. Greater public trust.

  • Drastic reduction in complaint-to-action SLA timelines
  • Eliminates misrouting — every complaint reaches the right desk
  • Builds a searchable grievance intelligence database
  • Restores citizen trust in transparent, accountable governance
